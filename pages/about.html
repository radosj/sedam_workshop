About SEDAM Workshop

Crafting exceptional furniture with passion, precision, and artistic vision since 2006.

Our Story

Founded in 2006, SEDAM Workshop emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create furniture that transcends mere functionality and becomes a statement of artistry and craftsmanship.

What started as a small atelier in Paris has grown into a renowned workshop known for its meticulous attention to detail and innovative design approach. Our journey began with a commitment to preserving traditional woodworking techniques while embracing contemporary aesthetics.
